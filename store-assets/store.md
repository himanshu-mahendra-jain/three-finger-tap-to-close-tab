Close the current tab with a three-finger tap, middle click, or toolbar click.

Three Finger Tap to Close Tab is a lightweight, open-source extension that provides a quick and simple way to close the current browser tab.

Three-finger tap or middle-click anywhere on a webpage outside links and editable fields to close the current tab. You can also click the extension icon in the toolbar to close the tab.

Middle-clicking links keeps the browser's normal behavior, so you can continue opening links in new tabs as usual.

KEY FEATURES

• Close the current tab with a three-finger tap
• Close the current tab with a middle click
• Close the current tab with a toolbar click
• Preserves normal middle-click behavior on links
• Avoids closing tabs while interacting with editable fields
• No popup menus or configuration

TOUCHPAD SETUP

Your touchpad and operating system must be configured so that a three-finger tap generates a middle click.

Once configured, three-finger tap on a webpage outside links and editable fields to close the current tab.

PRIVACY

• No analytics or tracking
• No data collection or browsing history collection
• No network requests
• No stored browsing activity or preferences

All tab-closing actions happen locally in your browser.
